SDR Activities

  • Qualify: ICP criteria, pain, compelling event

What Is a Compelling Event?

  • Regulatory deadline — must comply by a specific date
  • Contract expiry — current solution ends in 6 months
  • Market entry — launching new trading territory by a date
  • Budget cycle — approved budget, use it this year
  • Competitive pressure — rival just launched, must respond
  • ESG reporting deadline — board committed to year-end metrics

SQL Criteria — All Required (needs AE sign-off)

  • Contact is ICP (right company, country, company size)
  • Pain clearly identified and articulated
  • Compelling event exists within 12 months
  • Path to Economic Buyer identified (or EB met)
  • Contact is actively engaged (responded, showed interest)
  • AE has had at minimum one live conversation

What Happens at SQL

  • AE creates Unqualified Deal in Deal Pipeline
  • Deal linked to the Lead record
  • lead_source + lead_source_detail carried over to Deal (attribution preserved)
  • Lead stays in SQL stage — NOT deleted

Key Automation Flows

🤖 Stage Transition Auto-Summary (Phase 2 build)

SDR moves lead to new stage ↓ HubSpot workflow fires on stage change → Webhook to Zapier / Make ↓ Fetch from HubSpot: contact + company + lead properties + full activity timeline ↓ Send to Claude API with stage-specific prompt template: Connected entry → "Outreach Summary: what was tried, what connected, qualifying signals" Meeting Booked → "AE Handoff Brief: who they are, pain, compelling event, history, next step" SQL entry → "Full qualification summary: all SUGAARR criteria + SDR history" Closed Lost → "Closure summary: what was tried, why it closed, re-engagement context" ↓ Structured note created on Lead record via HubSpot API Slack to SDR / AE: "Summary note ready for [Company] → [link]"

🔄 Re-engagement Flow (Closed Lost → New Lead)

Lead closed as Closed Lost · recontact_date set ↓ Time passes... recontact_date is reached ↓ Workflow fires: NEW Lead record created for same contact → is_reactivated = true → Associated to original Closed Lost Lead record → Stage = New Lead → Original close reason surfaced in SDR task ↓ SDR task: "🔄 Re-engage [Company] — closed [date], reason: [reason] — [link to original]" Original Closed Lost record stays closed permanently — no backwards movement

🤖 PhantomBuster Enrichment Flow

Thomas runs PhantomBuster LinkedIn scraper → Input: HubSpot Company records (~200 ICP utilities) → Target roles: Innovation Manager / Head of Product / Trading Lead / Energy Manager / Head of Energy Trading ↓ Two outputs: ICP List (enriched company data) + Persona List (contacts ranked by role fit) ↓ HubSpot integration: Contact created / enriched → Lead auto-created → Stage = New Lead SDR task: "New enriched lead — [Name] at [Company] — [LinkedIn link]"

🌐 Inbound Lead Flow

Contact fills website calculator OR books demo via HubSpot Meetings ↓ Lead auto-created: Website Calculator → Stage = New Lead, lead_source = Inbound — Website Calculator Demo Booking → Stage = Meeting Booked, lead_source = Inbound — Demo Booking ↓ Default SDR assigned · SDR task: "🔥 Inbound lead — respond within 24h — [link]" Slack: same message ⚠️ To confirm: which AE gets default inbound assignment?

🤝 AE Handoff (Meeting Booked → SQL)

Lead reaches Meeting Booked ↓ Task to AE: "📅 [Name] at [Company] — discovery call [date] — [link]" Slack to AE: same · Auto-summary note generated (Phase 2) ↓ AE reviews pain_identified + auto-summary before the call ↓ AE creates Unqualified Deal in Deal Pipeline → lead_source + lead_source_detail carried to Deal · Lead stays in SQL
